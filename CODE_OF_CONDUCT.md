# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment.

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Avoid offensive language or behavior.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via issues or email.
Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warning or removal.
